Eclipse DemoCamp and Maker Faire Trondheim 2014

 28 August 2014    keilw  

After a great Eclipse DemoCamp Trondheim last year, I was invited to talk about UOMo, JSR 363 and the Quantified Self at Eclipse Luna DemoCamp Trondheim

We continued with a practical exercise of measuring a few "Pints" in the city. Interestingly after my "Measure for Measure" themed talk the pub was named "Macbeth" just like the other (probably more famous) play by William Shakespeare;-)

The following 2 days, the Force (in Newton;-D) was very strong with me, when I presented the improved version of Heart of Glass from last year's JavaOne Raspberry Pi Challenge at Trondheim Maker Faire.

I offered to measure his pulse and heart rate, but he didn't want to get out of his body armor;-D
